Welcome to HabitBuilder!

HabitBuilder app allows you to form new habits by tracking them and setting goals through challenges. The experience is gamified by creating a jigsaw puzzle that is unlocked once you complete a challenge.

The app was built using Express.js + MongoDB + HTML5/Bootstrap. Its current version doesn't support user authentication.

To use the app:

  • Click on "Create Habit" button and fill out the information
  • Start logging the units by simply clicking on the puzzle pieces
  • To delete a habit, click on "Delete Habit" dropdown menu
  • To update habit's title, click on "Rename" button
  • Once you opened the puzzle, the award is displayed on Awards page

The project is part of the CS5610 Web Development class at the Northeastern University.
